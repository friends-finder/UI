Features

Our goal at Friend Finder is to provide you, the user, with a unique experience unlike any other. In order to deliver the best experience, we've implemented a vast array of diverse features to facilitate the creation of new memories and help you connect with new and interesting people!

Tailored to your interests

Upon account creation, you will be guided through the process of selecting an array of questions to survey as many of your interests as possible. You will be able to see people using friend finder's filtering feature to determine whether or not you would like to request a match. From there, you can select 'Match' or 'Move on". Clicking "match" sends a message to that person to notify them that you are interested in meeting them. You can see a page of users who've requested a match and either reciprocate with a "match or move on".

Unique timelines and messaging

Once matched, you can use the messaging app built in and start chatting with one another and coordinate times/places to meet up to casually make new friends! The Timeline feature allows you to share photos/videos from recent fun hangouts to extend an open invitation for new users in the area to join during the next event.

Personality traits

Have you ever been worried that you and the person you meet won't have quite the right chemistry? Avoid personality clash with the unique Personality Traits feature! Choose from an array of personality types based on a short survey and find out with personality type best suits you. Only meet people you'll know will be great!
